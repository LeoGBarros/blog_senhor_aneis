About Blog

Welcome to Adventures in Middle-earth!

Leonardo Guedes Barros - Campinas/SP.

I created this blog as a way to share my passion for the incredible universe of *The Lord of the Rings*, created by J.R.R. Tolkien. From the first time I read the books and watched the movies, I was fascinated by the epic stories of courage, friendship, and adventure. This blog is the perfect space to explore theories, trivia, character analysis, and much more about Middle-earth!

In addition to my passion for Tolkien, I also have a great interest in developing web applications. I enjoy combining my creativity with the logic of programming to create dynamic and interactive solutions, like this very blog! For me, web development is an art that allows ideas to become reality, creating platforms that connect people and content.

I hope that here you find something new and inspiring about Tolkien's work and, at the same time, appreciate the effort behind the development of this platform!
